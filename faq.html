What types of paintings do you offer?
We offer a variety of paintings, including landscapes, portraits, and abstract art.
How can I purchase a painting?
You can purchase a painting by visiting our online store and selecting the painting you like. Follow the checkout process to complete your purchase.
How do I care for my paintings?
To care for your paintings, keep them away from direct sunlight, maintain a stable temperature and humidity, and dust them gently with a soft brush or microfiber cloth.
Can I request a custom painting?
Absolutely! We offer custom painting services. Contact us with your ideas, preferences, and specifications, and we'll work with you to create a unique piece of art.
Are the paintings signed by the artist?
Yes, all our paintings are signed by the artist. The signature is usually located in a discreet yet visible area on the front or back of the canvas.
What materials are used in the paintings?
We use high-quality materials such as professional-grade canvases, archival paints, and varnishes to ensure the longevity and durability of our paintings.
Can I return a painting if I'm not satisfied?
Yes, we have a return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact us within a specified timeframe for information on the return process and possible refunds.
Are the colors of the paintings accurate in the photos?
While we strive to represent the colors accurately, please note that the appearance may vary slightly due to different monitor settings. We recommend checking multiple images and contacting us for additional details if needed.
Can I commission a replica of a sold painting?
While we don't replicate sold paintings exactly, we can create a similar custom piece inspired by the sold artwork. Contact us for more details on commissioning a unique piece.
Are your paintings ready to hang?
Yes, our paintings are typically stretched on a wooden frame and come with hanging hardware. They are ready to hang and enjoy right out of the box.
